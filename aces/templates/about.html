About

Origin :

The whole reason we have made this website is to solve a problem and to help society.
Every year we waste money buying new text books and once we're promoted to the next year and we're done with them, we throw those books away or burn them, which causes pollution.

Solution :

Though this website we hope that people can sell and buy old textbooks or any other books, so that the books will be reused by those who are in search of them.

Customers :

This website is specialy built keeping students in mind who are either stuck between buying new books or borrowing library books.
However this website is open to anyone willing to but books.

Please note that for now this is a non-profit.

Developed by a team of full-stack developers.
